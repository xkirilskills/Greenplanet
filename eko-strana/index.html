Green Environment

“Green Environment” relates to the concerns for environmental conservation and improved health of the environment. This includes supporting practices like informed consumption, conservation practices and investment in renewable energy.

What is a greener planet?

“Greenness” often corresponds to the planet's ability to absorb carbon dioxide, the primary greenhouse gas that drives climate change. The more leaves, the more photosynthesis, a chemical reaction that gobbles up CO2. That's the good news in global greening: It's helping offset some of the impacts of climate change

How do you make a place greener?

  • Set up a composting system.
  • Switch to energy-efficient light bulbs.
  • Invest in reusable alternatives to single-use plastics.
  • Install solar panels.

How to make the earth green again?

Plant a tree

Planting a tree in your garden is one of the best way to reduce the air pollution. The trees absorb the carbon diopside and produce oxygen, by improving the air of the cities with positive effects on the health. More trees means cleaner air
